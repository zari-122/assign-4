THE FOUNDER
To keep up with modern times and the latest marketing and allied techniques the CAR DEALS was founded in the year 1966 at 104 Hotel Metropole Karachi, by Mr. Mohammad Hussain Dhanji, a perfect soft spoken, humble, modest person standing as a pioneer in Automotive Industry. An eminent well known national personality and leader of trading community.
He made an energetic and painstaking efforts to upgrade the CAR DEALS in to a grand showroom initially at I.I Chundigar Road, then P.E.C.H.S now in the posh area on main Clifton road and D.H.A Islamabad. His selfless, relentless pursuit and devotion of finance and untiring efforts towards community upliftment and social welfare for betterment of trade association are legendary. As the third generation of Dhanji family joined the group, the business expanded immensely and the turnover of CAR DEALS increased many folds.
(Late) Mr. Mohammad Hussain Dhanji
MESSAGE FROM SHABBIR HUSSAIN DHANJI
As the business entered into the second generation of CAR DEALS. Mr. Shabbir Hussain Dhanji who after completing his Automobile Sales Promotion course from American Motors, Detroit, USA; joined the family business and ventured into new areas within the car trade such as direct import of luxury cars, leasing & supply to corporate sector and multinational companies. He diversified the business towards international trading, property and assembling of cars in Pakistan in collaboration with Chinese and American counterpart.
In the recent years due to good referrals by our permanent clients we have seen a significant rise in demand for our services. As such, we have embarked upon an expansion phase, which has made our presence felt on the trade horizon and has earned us a distinctive name of being the most trusted House to deal with. Mr. Shabbir socially a very well connected and an active member of Karachi Chamber Of Commerce & Industry, Karachi Gymkhana, Karachi Club, Karachi Business Club, MEM, KPSIJ, and JIBA International Karachi Chapter.
Shabbir Hussain Dhanji CEO
MESSAGE FROM ALI ASGHAR DHANJI
As the business entered into the third generation of CAR DEALS, Mr. Ali Asghar Dhanji who graduated from The University of Buffalo, USA. On his return did his internship with Standard Chartered Bank Karachi. He followed the footsteps of his grandfather (Late) Mr. Mohammad Hussain Dhanji and father Mr. Shabbir Hussain Dhanji, soon earned the trust and confidence of our customers who showed loyalty in him as well. Mr. Ali has an excellent knack for communicating with his clients, his clear vision, straight forward thinking and decision making has given him high respect and sound market reputation.
Like his father he is also a sportsman and have won many badminton tournaments in Pakistan and abroad. He also takes part in social activities and supports the needy in their hour of need.
Ali Asghar Dhanji CFO

CAR DEALS MOTOR TRAINING INSTITUTE:
The training institute is housed under one roof at our Clifton showroom continuously operational on terms and conditions printed on the enrolment form. We have the honor of training on specially equipped dual controlled latest model cars more than 50,000 students till date. The present facility turns out approximately 100 students both male and female trained drivers each month. We are currently seeking an amnesty plot for formally developing an academic institute initially in Karachi.
